Manifesto for Devops culture

We both focus on applications stability and scalabity, therefore we intent to constantly make them easier and faster to build, test, package, deploy and operate

Develop automated and scalable scripts based on shared strategies

instead of operating manually

Monitor all metrics in real-time in shared and standard dashboards

instead of relying on local and specific data analysis tools

Participate, involve, share and interact with other team members

instead of keeping mehods, tools and information private

Expect the unexpected and get ready for worst scenarios

instead of focusing on ideal behaviors and being reactive
